Our Story

An NPO Azerbaijani Women in Science (AWiS) was founded by Gunel Aghabayli in June 2020 with the noble aim to create a strong community of Azerbaijani females united by STEM and STEM-related disciplines.

Mrs. Gunel was hugely inspired by the heritage of the Azerbaijani philanthropist H. Z. Taghiyev, who sponsored the building of the first secular school for Muslim girls in the whole Middle East and Russian Empire and who strongly advocated for educating Azerbaijani women. Taghiyev said: “When you educate a boy, you get one educated person. When you educate a girl, you will get an educated family” and we could not agree more!

Lessons at the Empress Alexandra Russian Muslim Boarding School for Girls, sponsored by H. Z. Taghiyev, 1911.

AWiS Board Members

Gunel Aghabayli

CEO, Director

Gunel is a Doctoral Researcher at the School of Engineering’s Institute for Energy Systems. She has a Bachelor’s degree in Applied Chemistry from Moscow State University. In 2015, Gunel received a government scholarship to pursue her Master’s degree in Chemical Engineering at the University of Birmingham and following this, began her Ph.D. in the School of Engineering at the University of Edinburgh.

Ravan Mammadov

Vice Chair & Business Development Manager

Process & Project Engineer with 8 years of engineering systems experience within oil & gas facilities, exclusively on projects for BP and SOCAR. Have a working knowledge of international codes and standards, including API, ASME, ISO, NACE, ANSI, ISA, BP speciﬁc speciﬁcations, and guidance practices. Motivated and dynamic professional with upstream and demonstrated expertise in Brownﬁeld and Greenﬁeld developments through all design life cycle stages comprising Conceptual, FEED, Detailed, and Construction.

Fidan Ismiyeva Miriyeva

Vice Chair and Design Manager

Fidan is an experienced digital and industrial product designer currently working in Berlin. She graduated from the Faculty of Industrial Product Design at the Middle East Technical University of Turkey in 2013-2017 and obtained her Design and Engineering MSc degree from the Politecnico di Milano University in Italy with the "Invest Your Talent in Italy" Scholarship in 2020. During her studies, she worked as a designer in well-known corporate companies, start-ups, and design studios.
