AWiS Network & Support

Since June 2020 we strongly rely on the AWiS team members and we belive that without the team effort and our local and international network there would be neither AWiS we have today nor hundreds of positively impacted girls’ lives!

We invite you to join to our network, or as we call it “AWiS Hub” to create even more educational projects and positively contribute to the issues of Gender Inequality and Qualitative Education.

Read the available roles with descriptions below and fill in the form. Our team will reach out to you as soon as we can.

We want Azerbaijani females to be successful in the following three areas:

Volunteer

  • If you are interested in gaining volunteering experience in project execution, SMM, or design, AWiS might be a right place for you!
  • We are always eager to welcome responsible talents, who are ready to spare their time and effort to contribute to our mission!

Partner

  • Partner with us if you are an organization or an entrepreneur and are considering to implement an educational program.
  • AWiS has a reliable portfolio and references of local and international communities to prove that you won’t regret doing a project together!

Mentor

  • AWiS staff and friends are mentoring schoolgirls from the regions as well as female university students from STEM disciplines.
  • We invite you to positively impact to the lives of youth with your professional expertise and experience in industry or academia.

Donor

  • Currently, AWiS sustain the activity thanks to the grant projects and service fees for other orgs, while staff works on voluntary basis.
  • Through donations we will be able to do even more for Azerbaijani school girls’ and young women’s education and development

Fellow (coming soon)

  • We at AWiS believe in the power of community and knowledge-sharing.
  • Become our fellow and benefit form AWiS perks for professionals and experts on periodic subscription!
  • Subscribe the earliest to know the updates and get the best deal!

Other role

  • If you did not find the suitable role, feel free to reach out to us anyways to share suggestion, story or consern about AWiS, education or career.
  • Rest assured we read all messages and answer all of them!
