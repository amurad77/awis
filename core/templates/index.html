Azerbaijani Women in Science (AWiS) aims to bring more Azerbaijani girls into STEM and STEM-related disciplines and contribute to building educated Azerbaijani society.

Join our mailing list to receive updates on our new projects, special opportunities and more!

    We want Azerbaijani females to be successful in the following three areas:

    Qualitative Education

    • AWiS is committed to drawing attention to quality education in Azerbaijan and popularizing lifelong learning concept among girls.
    • We collect and share funded educational opportunities for Azerbaijani schoolgirls and female students with STEM background.

    Personal Development

    • AWiS’ mission is to encourage self-development from school age and becoming better every day.
    • We regularly share posts on skills development and self-improvement tactics to assist young females in progressing forward with their goals.

    Satisfactory Career

    • AWiS strives to inspire young females to pursue their career dreams regardless of cultural stigmas and society pressure.
    • We invite experts and professionals from STEM disciplines as mentors and publish entry level career opportunities for Azerbaijani youth.

    Divercity is the key to solving our country’s biggest problems

    Created in Summer 2020 by Gunel Aghabayli, a young award-winning scientist, AWiS is a NPO which aims to increase the number of women in various fields of science and build a community of educated women in the country.

    About Us

    Being proactive is what makes AWiS exceptional

    AWiS proves to be one of the most motivated NPO organizations, driven to involve as many schoolgirls from Azerbaijani regions as possible to participate in tailored educational programs and scientific projects.
